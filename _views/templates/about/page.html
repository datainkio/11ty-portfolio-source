⦿ What I Do

20+ years of award-winning work

Collaboration

I work closely with user researchers to identify key questions and hypotheses that reveal critical insights into human behavior. These insights directly shape the design strategies I develop, guiding designers toward more impactful solutions. By bringing a nuanced human perspective, I help engineers translate possibilities into tangible outcomes that put people first.

Strategy

I spend as much time sweating UI pixels and UX flows as I do sitting in workshops and boardrooms, discussing clients’ user needs, technical constraints, product strategy, and business objectives.

Direction

As a design lead, I am often hands-on encouraging my teams to play, remix, and look at things sideways - using customer and business insights, brand, storytelling, and human-centered design to create products and services that meet real human needs.

Roots in anthropology

My education is in social anthropology. As a student I explored the ongoing dialog between language, tool use, and human behavior. Those years spent studying the interrelationships between different models of the human being continue to shape my values and methods as a designer.

Collaboration

I apply this expertise across a wide range of domains, d elivering solutions for global brands, museums, artists, musicians, hospitals, schools, and even the federal government. The audiences and business goals change, but the priority of maintaining focus on human-centered design remains constant.

It is work with a real impact. As the design manager for federal projects with Bixal, my teams and I increased equitable access to federal benefits and services for 330 million Americans, connected small businesses with $4.2 billion in funding, helped reduce the number of annual cases of foodborne illness by 7 million, and helped establish the United States Web Design System. As a developer and product designer with IBM Interactive, I led the design for the company's first application of Watson AI to healthcare, helped design and build both the world's first global platform for democratizing access to pediatric expertise, and helped design and build the first platform uniting urban data from cities across the globe.

Contributions
