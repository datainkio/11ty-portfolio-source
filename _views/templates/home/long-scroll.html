Russell S. Lebo

I am a human-experience designer.

I team up with user researchers, UI designers, engineers, and accessibility experts to deliver award-winning digital products and services that answer real human needs.

My work appears in the questions researchers ask, the strategies that lead designers to solutions, and the human perspective that helps engineers realize what is possible.

As a design manager for $65.5M in federal contracts, I connected American entrepeneurs with billions of dollars in funding during COVID, increased food safety, and increased equitable acccess to federal benefits and services for millions of Americans.

As a product design lead, I designed and prototyped IBM's first application of AI to healthcare and gave biologists tools to understands the genetic causes of disease.

As a developer and interaction designer, I created award-winning cinematic experiences for artists, museums, world-famous DJs, and world-wide brands.
